## Project Background

This project is a teamwork piece from the Alpine Field School at Dresden University of Technology (TU Dresden). We selected the Planai and Hochwurzen areas in the Schladming mountains to design a panoramic tourist map for visitors engaging in summer outdoor activities.

To avoid overwhelming tourists with complex geographic information, we eliminated redundant details in our design, focusing on our core objectives: clarity, simplicity, and visual relaxation. The map retains only the elements most relevant to summer tourism, such as hiking trails, cable car stations, restaurants, and viewing platforms.

## Field Observation

The visual characteristics and atmosphere of the map are inseparable from true physical perception. Before mapping, we personally visited Planai and Hochwurzen for field observations. Standing physically amidst the mountains helped us better capture the local topographical variations, vegetation distribution, and sense of spatial scale.

## Visual Design and Cartography

To achieve the ideal "hand-drawn panoramic map" effect, we adopted an innovative workflow integrating traditional GIS, AI assistance, and vector illustration:

* **3D Perspective Construction:** First, we extracted basemap data from OpenStreetMap (OSM) and imported it into ArcGIS Pro. By adjusting the camera angle and pitch in the 3D scene, we established a bird's-eye panoramic perspective.
* **AI-Assisted Stylization:** We innovatively utilized AI tools to assist in converting the exported basemap into a style with black-and-white hand-drawn textures, and manually corrected the spatial geometric distortions that occurred during the generation process.
* **Visual Hierarchy and Layout:** In the later stages of processing with Adobe Illustrator and Procreate, we kept the base terrain in a black-and-white texture to highlight the geomorphological features. Meanwhile, we used green (representing nature and harmony) and orange (adding contrast and vitality) to draw icons for key facilities. This contrast between cool and warm colors allows core tourist information to stand out against a relaxed background.
