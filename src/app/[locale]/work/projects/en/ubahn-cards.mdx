## Project Background

Since moving to Vienna, I have been captivated by the city's stunning urban landscape. At the same time, I developed a strong interest in cardistry and began collecting various themed decks. This sparked an idea: why not combine "playing cards" with "city exploration"—two things I truly love?

The core concept is **"Collect & Connect"**: it is both a standard deck of 54 playable cards and a modular subway map puzzle. By breaking the limitations of traditional paper maps, I aimed to create a playful and memorable creative souvenir for tourists exploring the city.

## Visual Design

The entire project was primarily created using **Adobe Illustrator** for vector drawing and layout. The most significant cartographic challenge was ensuring continuity and precise segmentation. I needed to accurately cut the complex Vienna U-Bahn network into 54 individual card grids while maintaining:

* **Functionality:** Each card retains clear suits and numbers in the corners (e.g., King of Hearts, 7 of Spades) to ensure its utility for traditional card games.
* **Connectivity:** Using Illustrator’s rigorous grid and guide system, I ensured that when all cards are assembled in a specific order, the subway lines at the edges align perfectly to form a complete U-Bahn map.

For the card faces and packaging, I extracted the iconic colors of Vienna's five core subway lines (U1 Red, U2 Purple, U3 Orange, U4 Green, U6 Brown). **You can try dragging the card stack on the right to explore different cards:**

## Physical Product

This project did not stop at the digital concept stage. To experience the full workflow of cultural and creative product design, I personally contacted manufacturers for physical prototyping.

From adjusting bleed lines and verifying CMYK print color modes to selecting card stock materials and surface finishes, I navigated the journey from "blueprint" to "physical object." Receiving the high-quality deck not only validated the precision of the Illustrator-based modular design but also gave me a profound sense of accomplishment.
