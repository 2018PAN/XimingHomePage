## Introduction

This is a fun cartography exercise we completed in Ramsau am Dachstein, Styria, Austria.

During our hike, we recorded key landmarks along the way, such as supermarkets, farms, and rivers. Afterwards, these elements were illustrated into this hand-sketched map.
